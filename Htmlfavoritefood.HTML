Favorite food #1 Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a desi food made with meat and rice. Biryani can be made with Chicken, Beef, fish, Lamb, and shrimp. It takes a long time to make Biryani so we usually eat it in holidays.

Why I like this food

  1. I love chicken biryani because it is made with chicken and it has really good seasoning.
  2. The rice on chicken biryani tastes really good.
  3. The Chicken is very soft so its easy to chew.

Favorite food #2 Bangladeshi Fried Fish

This Fried Fish is Fried with oil and onion. there are many kind of spices added in the fish while its being fried. It tastes kinda sourish and spicy.

Why I like this food

  1. I tastes really good with regular rice.
  2. The fish is cooked really well
  3. It's kinda crispy

Favorite food #3 chicken seekh kebab.

Chicken seekh kabob is made with chicken keema. The keema is attached to a rod and later on grilled or fried on a frying pan to make sure it is well cocked. Chicken seehkh kebab is juicy, delicate, and seasoned.

Why I like this food

  1. Chicken Seekh Kebab tastes really good with naan.
  2. You can also eat it with plain rice.
  3. The kebab is very well season and it is very easy to chew.
